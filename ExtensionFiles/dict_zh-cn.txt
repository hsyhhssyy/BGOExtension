Each civilization loses 4 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per discontent worker. All civilizations with the most discontent workers also returns 1 blue token to the box. If no one has discontent workers, this event has no effect.

Emigration
Each civilization loses half of its discontent workers, rounded up (return them to your yellow bank).
Iconoclasm
Each civilization with a leader that is not from the current age must remove that leader from play.
Independence Declaration
The weakest civilization loses 1 colony (the player choses which one).
International Agreement
The strongest civilization may immediately take up to 5 <img alt="civil action" title="Civil Action" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/ca.gif"> in cards by giving up its next chance to take a political action. Replenish the card row. This may end the Age.

Each civilization scores <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> equal to the amount of <img alt="science" title="Science" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/science.png"> it produces.

Each player chooses one of his or her completed wonder from Age A or I and turns it face down. These wonders lose their effects and produce 2 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> instead.

The strongest scores 3 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> and gains 1 population. The weakest civilization loses 3 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> and 1 population.

Each civilization scores <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> equal to the amount of <img alt="food" title="Food" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/food.png"> it produces with a bonus of 4 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> if this exceeds consumption.
Impact of Architecture
Each civilization scores 1 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per level of each of its urban buildings.
Impact of Balance
Each civilization scores <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> equal to double the amount of whatever it produces the least (<img alt="science" title="Science" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/science.png">, <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png">, <img alt="food" title="Food" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/food.png">, or <img alt="resource" title="Resource" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/resource.png">). Ignore corruption and consumption.

Each civilization scores 3 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per colony.
Impact of Competition
Each civilization scores 1 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per level of each of its military units and <img alt="arena" title="Arena" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/arena.png">.

Each civilization scores 2 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per <img alt="civil action" title="Civil Action" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/ca.gif"> and 1 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per <img alt="military action" title="Military Action" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/ma.gif">.

Impact of Industry
Each civilization scores <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> equal to the amount of <img alt="resource" title="Resource" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/resource.png"> its mines produce. (Ignore any production from other sources)
Impact of Population
Each civilization scores 2 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per content worker above 10.
Impact of Progress
Each civilization scores 2 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> per level of each of its government and special (blue) technologies.

Each civilizations score <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> according to the relative amount of <img alt="science" title="Science" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/science.png"> it produces:<br> 15/10/5/0 for 4-player<br> 14/7/0 for 3-player<br> 10/0 for 2-player
Impact of Strength
Each civilizations score <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> according to their relative <img alt="strength" title="Strength" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/strength.png">:<br> 15/10/5/0 for 4-player<br> 14/7/0 for 3-player<br> 10/0 for 2-player
Impact of Technology
Each civilization scores 4 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> for each of its level III technologies (level II in Advanced game).
Impact of Variety
Each civilization scores 2 <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> for each type of unit, urban building and special (blue) technology.
Impact of Wonders
Each civilization scores <img alt="culture" title="Culture" class="iconeTexte" src="images/tta7/culture.png"> for each wonders:<br> 5 for age A<br> 4 for age I<br> 3 for age II<br> 2 for age III
